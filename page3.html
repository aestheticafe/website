INTERIOR

The design of the store is inspired  by  a modernized antique style cafe which consists of wood furnitures and paintings . Its furnitures have a touch of brown and black. The place is spacious and it gives you a relaxing vibe like when you are at your home. The cafe's soft music and dim lighting gave it a cozy ambience. 

ENVIRONMENT 
When it comes to its environment, the inside of cafe is fully air conditioned and clean. The staff are good, friendly, and polite. The atmosphere is quite and peaceful, that is good for meet ups. the outside of cafe is clean.

INTERIOR

The interior of the cafe is more on combination of vintage and forest style consisting of antiques. There are many flowers, trees, books, mini vintage toy cars that are used as decorations.

ENVIRONMENT

CAFE CONGRESO

Adress: Paseo Del Congreso Street, Amlolos, Bulacan
Hours Opened: Monday to Sunday - 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM

INTERIOR

The interior design of the cafe is inspired by "Grand Budafest Hotel" movie. Pastel clors make this cafe unique from any other cafe. The colors that were generally used are pastel pink and mint green with subtle accents of golden brown and teal making it perfect for the people who are pastel color lovers. The design is also somewhat vintage because of the cafe equipments and other decorations. The color of the sofas and chairs matches with the color of the walls which is pastel pink.

ENVIRONMENT
The cafe has a cozy ambience. The music choice matches the elegant design of the cafe.The cafe is also air-conditioned and the temperature is just right. The overall mood is relaxing. The cafe also maintained its cleanliness and it looks pleasing to the eyes of the people.

INTERIOR

The interior of the cafe is elegant-looking. Its wall and chairs is painted with black which makesit simple yet modern. Most of the Things are in color black or white including the chandelier which is very elegant. It is perfect for the people who love the color black

ENVIRONMENT
The music matches with the mood of the cafe.Thus place is good for studying or catching ups with friends because there's not much people eating here.
