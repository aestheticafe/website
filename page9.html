CAFE CONGRESO FOOD REVIEW

Cafe Caramel

The Cafe Caramel costs 140 pesos and it tastes just right for the one who are not use to drink bitter coffee. The taste of the caramel stands out more than the taste of the coffee. The caramel itself gives the coffee a sweeter taste.

Signature Cheeseburger

It costs 200 pesos but the price is really worth it. It has two layers of goo-quality patties, it also comes with cheese and plenty of vegetables. Chips are also included in the plate and it complements the taste of the cheeseburger.

Cafe Mocha

Their Cafe Mocha costs 140 pesos and the cup is wide so the servingwas big enough for one person. The taste is slightly bitter at the first sip while the after taste is smewhat sweet.
