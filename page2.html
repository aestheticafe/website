STARBUCKS

We ordered Cafe Americano, Cafe Latte, and Original Glazed Doughnut. A drink or food from Starbucks is not affordable but the quality and taste are excellent.

CAFE CONGRESO

We ordered Cafe Mocha and Cafe Caramel for our drinks. For the foods, we chose their signature cheeseburger. The drinks are somewhat affordable compared to the other cafes but their food is a bit pricey. The cafe serves good food that is worth the price.

COFFEE PROJECT

WENCE DESSERT CAFE

We ordered Thai Iced Coffee and Cappuccino Frappe, together with the Baked Macaroni and Creamy Carbonara. The prices are very affordable and the taste is superb.
