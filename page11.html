WENCE DESSERT CAFE 
FOOD REVIEW

Creamy Carbonara

The Creamy Carbonara costs 109 pesos. The carbonara has mushroom, bits of bacons, ham, cream, pasta and cheese for the ingridients. The creamy sauce is perfect, it's not salty and it's also not that sweet. The sause made the carbonara delicious. It's not the best carbonara that I have tried but it's definitely delicious. It was filling and satisfying.

Thai Coffee and Cappuccino Frappe

Thai Iced Coffee costs 90 pesos. The serving is bigger than what we have expected. Its taste is fine, the bitterness of the coffee blends with the sweetness. Their Thai Iced Coffee tastes like an ordinary coffee but it's worth it considering its size.  We bought Cappuccino Frappe, this costs 69 pesos. The Cappuccino Frappe has its coffee taste and it's a little bit sweet. The price is worth it because the size of the glass is big.

Baked Macaroni

The Baked Macaroni in special size costs 109 pesos while the regular size is 85 pesos. I ordered the special size and it has much more cheese in it which made the dish more delicious. The Baked Macaroni was sweet and not that salty, the taste of the cheese is perfect. This has always been my favorite food from the menu of Wence Dessert Cafe.
