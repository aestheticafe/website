STARBUCKS FOOD REVIEW

Cafe Americano

Cafe Americano costs 130 pesos for a tall size. It has an intense bitter taste for someone who doesn't really drink pure coffe. It is a taste that is loved by elders because of its bitter and pure taste.

Cafe Latte

Cafe Latte costs 140 pesos for a tall size. Cafe Latte is just right for people who do not like the intense taste of the Cafe Americano. It has bitter taste but not as mush as Cafe Americano. It has a somewhat sweet taste and younger people would like it.

Honey Glazed Doughnut

Honey Glazed Doughnut costs 60 pesos. I think the price is worth it because the serving is bigger than what i have always expected. The texture of the doughnut is really soft and the glazed just melts in your mouth. The glazed adds sweetness to the coffee and it complements the bitter taste of it.
