COFFEENITY CO. FOOD REVIEW

Brownie Ala Mode

The brownies costs 60 pesos. The serving was quite small, but the plating was beautiful. The tastes of the brownie ala mode is a bit sweet, but the vanilla ice cream at the top complements the sweetness of the brownies. The texture of the brownies is  moist and it was perfect for me because I do not prefer dry textures when it comes to food.

Iced Latte and Iced Cappuccino

Iced Capuccino costs 110 pesos, the taste is bitter,it has a strong bitteerness and there's no sweetness present in this drink. Iced Latte costs 130 pesos. The serving was bigger than the Iced Capuccino. The Iced Latte has its pure coffee taste and a little bit of sweetness in it.

French Fries

We bought the french fries for 65 pesos and the serving is enough for two people. Their french fries is much more plain than what i have expected, but it tastes good. It isn't salty and it tastes good when dip in ketchup.
